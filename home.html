Welcome to Book Swap!

We are an online community where you can swap books with other book lovers all around New Zealand.

Why keep spending time and money shopping for new books when you can update your library here for free - and the books come to you!

How It Works

Step 1: Sign up or log in

Step 2: 'Sell' a book and earn a point

Step 3: Exchange your points to 'buy' a book!
